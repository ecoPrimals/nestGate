## 🏗️ Architecture Status

### **Core Components** ✅

- ✅ **Infant Discovery:** Zero-config, runtime-adaptive service discovery
- ✅ **Universal Adapter:** O(1) service connections, capability-based routing
- ✅ **Universal Storage:** ZFS abstraction with future backend support
- ✅ **Error Recovery:** Circuit breakers, retry logic, graceful degradation
- ✅ **Configuration System:** Constants module with environment support

### **Modern Rust Patterns** ✅

- ✅ `Arc<T>` for thread-safe sharing
- ✅ `RwLock<T>` for concurrent read access
- ✅ `Mutex<T>` for exclusive write access
- ✅ `Duration` for type-safe time handling
- ✅ `PathBuf` for safe filesystem paths
- ✅ `Result<T>` for error handling
- ✅ Pattern matching throughout
- ✅ Zero-copy where possible
