## Archive Context
These files represent the evolution of NestGate from development phase to production-ready system. They document the resolution of technical debt, achievement of key milestones, and the completion of major features. The files were archived as part of the documentation cleanup to maintain a clean root directory while preserving historical context.

## Current Status
As of July 15, 2024, NestGate has achieved production readiness with all major implementation gaps resolved. These archived files provide historical context for the development journey but are no longer needed for ongoing development or deployment.
